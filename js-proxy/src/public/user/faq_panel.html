Frequently Asked Questions
  • How to use this website?
    You should first create an account by providing credentials. Next use them to log in to the website. You should be redirected to the main page of the fashion shop template. Try to use this website as a normal shop, but keep in mind that it is the only example of a shop, so not all functions are available. In the background, the page will collect your mouse data and send it to our server. If you finish your session, just close the tab in your browser. You can return to the website in the future, but we kindly ask you to use the same credentials.
  • Why are you collecting my data?
    In order to prepare behavioral biometric solution in the basis of mouse biometrics, we need to collect various user data of many users to prepare machine learning model which next will be examined whether is applicable on the real commercial websites.
  • What about my data, is it secure?
    We ensure that the collected data will not be published in the thesis or in any public medium. We will use the data only for academic purposes. After completing the thesis the data will be deleted from any local storage and server resources.
  • Do you use cookies on this site?
    Yes, we do. These cookies are necessary to properly collect data and authenticate the user.
  • Who we are?
    We are students of AGH University of Science and Technology, Department of Computer Science, Electronics and Telecommunication, who are preparing the bachelor thesis.
